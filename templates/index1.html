Why use a Dupchecker?
Regardless of whether you intended to plagiarise or not, using another person's words without giving them proper credit is illegal. Even a phrase or two of unintentional plagiarism might have major repercussions. Plagiarism among students frequently results in a failing mark, academic probation, or even worse. Fortunately, a helpful tool exists. You can use Dupchecker to make sure that anything in your writing that isn't entirely original has been appropriately identified and cited.
